HOW IT REGULATES ITSELF
• CO₂ & methane trap heat; ice & clouds bounce it away.
• Warm wet rock scrubs CO₂ — the planet's own thermostat.
• Life eats CO₂, breathes out O₂; enough O₂ unlocks animals & land.
• Push too far either way → a frozen snowball or a boiled Venus.
